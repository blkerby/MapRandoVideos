Video Submission Guidelines

  • Video must be in uncompressed AVI format, captured directly from an accurate emulator.
  • Video must have the native resolution (256 x 224, 60.1 fps) and game audio only.
  • Use the Practice ROM to show inputs, and use InfoHUD to show other relevant info if applicable.
  • Set the frame counters to "INGAME".
  • Where relevant, pause and show the equipment screen at the end of the video.
Reach out on the #development channel of the Discord and we can help set up an account for you.
